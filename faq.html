Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What kind of plants do you sell on your online nursery website?

A: We offer a wide variety of indoor and outdoor plants, including succulents, flowering plants, houseplants, herbs, trees, and shrubs. Our selection is constantly growing, so check back frequently for new arrivals.

Q: How are your plants shipped?

A: We take great care in packaging and shipping our plants to ensure they arrive in excellent condition. We use specialized packaging materials and ship via fast and reliable carriers to minimize transit time.

Q: Do you offer any guarantees on your plants?

A: Yes, we stand behind the quality of our plants and offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, please let us know and we'll do everything we can to make it right.

Q: What if I'm not sure which plants are right for my home or garden?

A: Our team of knowledgeable plant experts is always available to answer any questions you may have about plant care and selection. You can also browse our website for helpful tips and information.

Q: How do I care for the plants I purchase from your website?

A: We provide detailed care instructions for each plant on our website, including information on watering, light requirements, soil type, and more. If you have any additional questions or concerns, our plant experts are always available to help.
